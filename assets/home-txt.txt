There are hundreds of thousands of craftable potions in Morrowind. Finding the most interesting ones manually is time consuming. This webapp is designed to ease the process of creating potions. It has features for finding 'optimal' potions, emulating the in-game potion menu, and getting information on the individual ingredients. Ingredients from the Tamriel Data mod (for use with Tamriel Rebuilt and Project Tamriel) are also optionally included.
Ingredients have up to four magical effects associated with them. Potions are mixtures of two, three, or four ingredients. If two (or more) ingredients in a potion share the same effects, these effects will be included in the resulting potion. A 'Mortar and Pestle' is required to create potions, and there are a few other tools than can improve the result. Your Alchemy, Intelligence, and Luck attributes affect the final result.
On the potion database page, you can select what effects you want, and a table will list all 'minimal' potions that have those effects. A 'minimal' potion is one where all the ingredients are necessary for the desired effects. For example, there are many potions that have the 'Blind' effect (e.g. 'Bristleback Leather', 'Durzog Meat', 'Adamantium Ore', 'Comberry'), but only two ingredients actually have this effect, so there is only one 'minimal' potion for 'Blind'. This is done to reduce the number of potions shown. Potions with more effects can then be built up by adding more effects. The example potion can be found by selecting both 'Blind' and 'Restore Magicka'. The ingredient's origin can be filtered as well. This is mainly for including/excluding ingredients from Tamriel Data. The resulting potions are ordered by positive effects descending and then negative effects ascending.
The potion maker page is simply a copy of the in-game potion making menu. Here, you input your stats, alchemy tools, and ingredients, and it will calculate the magnitude and duration of the effects of the resulting potion.
Once you have found some interesting potions, you'll want to actually make them. One problem is finding the ingredients. The ingredient info page lists important information about each ingredient, including its price, weight, and list of NPCs and exterior cells it can be found in.
